üö® Safety Guidelines

Essential safety information for Patagonian hiking

Weather Awareness

  • Check weather forecasts daily and be prepared for sudden changes
  • Patagonian weather is notoriously unpredictable
  • Always carry windproof and waterproof gear
  • Turn back if conditions deteriorate

Emergency Preparedness

  • Inform someone of your hiking plans and expected return
  • Carry a satellite communicator or emergency beacon
  • Know emergency contact numbers for each region
  • Carry a comprehensive first aid kit

River Crossings

  • Many trails require river crossings - assess conditions carefully
  • Cross early in the morning when water levels are lowest
  • Unbuckle pack waist belt before crossing
  • Use trekking poles for stability
